Artist: Marcus "Mako" Rivera

Marcus Rivera, known in the local art scene as "Mako," is a Chicago-based muralist and mixed-media artist whose work explores identity, community, and the raw energy of urban life. Drawing inspiration from the streets that raised him, Mako layers vivid color and bold linework to create pieces that feel both monumental and deeply personal. Organic and Sustainable Brews

Every cup we serve starts with a promise — to the farmers who grow our beans, to the land they tend, and to you. We source exclusively from certified organic, fair-trade cooperatives across Costa Rica, Ethiopia, and Colombia, ensuring that every harvest is grown without synthetic pesticides and that every farmer is paid a fair, living wage.

Our beans are small-batch roasted right here in Chicago, never more than 72 hours before they reach our brewers. We believe freshness isn't a luxury — it's the baseline. From light floral Ethiopian naturals to our rich, chocolatey Costa Rica dark roast, each profile is dialed in to bring out the best the bean has to offer.

This month we're featuring the Cultivate Costa Rica — a smooth, medium roast with notes of brown sugar, almond, and a clean citrus finish. Available as a drip, pour-over, or whole bean bag to take home. *Description made with generative AI*
